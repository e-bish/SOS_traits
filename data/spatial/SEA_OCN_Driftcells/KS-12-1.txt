Suquamish quad


Originating in a divergence zone at Point Bolin and ending in the 
embayment on the west side of Section 25, this drift cell exhibits 
a northwesterly net shore-drift. This drift direction is indicated
by groins and bulkheads trees and fallen across the beach displaying 
sediment accumulation on the south and east side, a 20 meter stream 
diversion to the northwest in Section 31, beach width increasing to 
the north and west, and a spit building to the northwest in section 25.
