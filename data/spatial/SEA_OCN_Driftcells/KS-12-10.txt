Suquamish quad

Originating in a divergence zone at Point Bolin and ending in the
embayment on the west side of Section 25, this drift cell exhibits a
northwesterly net shore-drift.  This drift direction is indicated by
groins and bulkheads displaying sediment accumulation on the south and
east side, a 20 meter stream diversion to the northwest in Section 31,
beach width increas-ing to the north and west, and a spit building to
the northwest in section 25.
