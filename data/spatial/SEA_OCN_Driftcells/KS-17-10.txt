Bremerton East quad


This drift cell originates at Point White and ends about 0.2 km west of
Lynnwood Center.  Northeastward net shore-drift is indicated by
sediment against the southwest side of a concrete boat ramp
approximately 0.5 km west of Lynnwood Center, as well as decreasing
sediment size to the northeast from cobbles and pebbles at Point White
to pebbles and sand at Lynnwood Center.
