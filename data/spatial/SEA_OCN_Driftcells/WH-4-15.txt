Eliza Island quad

This drift cell is located on the eastern side of Portage Island and
includes Brant Point.

Net shore drift is to the north and northwest.

The greatest fetch is 18 kilometers to the southeast.  The prevailing
and predominant winds are from the south and southeast.  The coast
changes from eroding bluffs at the south to a spit built to the
northwest, known as Brant Point.  Brant Point is located at the
northern end of the cell.  The sediment grades from cobbles at the
south to sand at the northwest.
